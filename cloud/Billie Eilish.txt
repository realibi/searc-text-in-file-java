Billie Eilish Pyrat Baird About'Connell was born on December 18, 2001 in Los Angeles in a creative family. Her mother Maggie Baird is an actress and her father Patrick O'Connell is a musician.

Billy has an older brother, Finneas O'Connell (he is 21 years old), and he is also fond of music and acting: he sings in the band The Slightlys, starred in the last season of the TV series "Glee" (Glee). Now he writes songs for his sister as well.
